Label Decorations

These preferences can be changed on 'Team>SVN>Label Decorations' properties page. This page allows enabling font and color decorations, previewing decoration result and getting references for decoration configuring. To change templates of text decoration refer to 'Text decorations' tab, and to turn on/off some of the icon decorations refer to 'Icons Decorations' tab.

Tip:

Please feel free to customize the font and icon decorations. Making your work comfortable is the most important way to improve your productivity.
